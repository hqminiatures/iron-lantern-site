I.

Our Philosophy

We believe the best adventures are dangerous, concise, and ready to run. No filler encounters. No pages of backstory your players will never hear. Just the stuff that makes the table go quiet.

Iron Lantern publishes for Shadowdark RPG and the broader OSR community — old-school rules, new-school design. Every module is built to hit the table fast and leave a mark.

I

Table-Ready

Open the book. Run the dungeon. No prep required.

II

Danger First

Real stakes. Real consequences. Torches burn out.

III

No Bloat

Every room earns its page count. Every word pulls weight.

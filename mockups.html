The Crimson Vault

— 4 —

Area 3: The Blood Chamber

The air grows thick with the smell of iron and decay. Crimson stains streak the walls, still wet despite the centuries that have passed. A low hum resonates from deeper within the vault.

At a Glance: This circular chamber once served as a ritual space for the cult. The stone floor is etched with sigils that pulse with faint amber light.

What's Here: Three stone altars ring the room, each bearing the skeletal remains of a sacrifice. Candles flicker with ghostly flames that cast no heat.

Hollow Cultist
AC: 14 | HP: 22 | ATK: +4 (1d8+2)
MV: near | S: +2, D: +1, C: +0
Special: Immune to fear, vulnerable to radiant damage

Treasure: Hidden beneath the central altar: a tarnished silver chalice (50gp) and a scroll of Dispel Magic.

Secret: DC 15 Intelligence check reveals a hidden passage behind the western altar leading to Area 4.
